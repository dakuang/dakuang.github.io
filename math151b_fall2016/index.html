Syllabus

  • QR factorization, least squares
    Why is QR factorization useful?
    What's the difference between Gram-Schmidt and Householder algorithms for QR?
    What's the difference between normal equation method and QR for discrete least squares?
    How to fit a polynomial to a continuous function?
    Implementation of Gram-Schmidt, Householder, normal equation method

  • Computing eigenvalues and eigenvectors
    How are eigenvalues and eigenvectors computed practically on a computer?
    Why does power method converge to the leading eigenvector?
    How to compare the speed of different algorithms?
    Implementation of power iteration, QR algorithm; Plotting the convergence

  • Solving nonlinear systems of equations
    When does Newton's method converge for nonlinear systems of equations?
    Implementation of Newton's method, Broyden's method
    Comparing the convergence between Newton's method and Broyden's method

  • Solving initial value problems for ODE
    What kind of ODE systems can be solved accurately? (stability)
    How to choose the step size h in Euler's method?
    How to improve the accuracy and stability of Euler's method? (Runga-Kutta; backward Euler)
    When does an explicit method for IVP have difficulty? (stiffness)
    Implementation of Euler's method, backward Euler's method; Plotting the convergence

  • Solving boundary value problems for ODE
    Transforming BVP to solving linear/nonlinear systems of equations (shooting method, finite difference)
    Transforming BVP to function fitting problems (collocation, Galerkin FEM)
    Implementation of finite difference, collocation
